## Privacy Policy for Syncore App ([dav.one.syncore](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=dav.one.syncore))

**Last updated: 18.05.2026**

**Dawid Wąsowski** built the "Syncore" app as a free app. This SERVICE is
provided by Dawid Wąsowski at no cost and is intended for use as is. This app
does not collect any data from its users. It does not require any personal
information, nor does it track or store any user activity.

### Information Collection and Use

The "Syncore" app does not collect any personal data from users. 
Furthermore, since no personal data is collected, no data is shared with, sold to, or transferred to any third parties. 

All SFTP connection credentials (host, port, username, password or private key) are stored locally on your device only, 
kept entirely within the secure SQLite database and encrypted system storage, 
and are never transmitted to the developer or any third party. 
Files are synchronized directly between your device and your own SFTP server — 
no data passes through the developer's infrastructure, 
and no intermediate proxies or third-party servers are used to relay your sync traffic.

### System Permissions

Syncore requests local storage access permissions to browse and read the folders selected for synchronization, 
and background battery exemption permissions to reliably execute automatic sync operations in the background. 
Storage access is utilized strictly locally and is never transmitted to external servers for tracking or any other purposes.

### Log Data

The "Syncore" app does not log any data from users. No usage data, diagnostic
information, or crash reports are sent to the developer.

### Cookies

The "Syncore" app does not use cookies.

### Service Providers

The "Syncore" app does not use any third-party services that may collect
information used to identify you. There are no analytics, advertising, or
crash-reporting SDKs included in the app.

### Security

The "Syncore" app does not store any personal data on the developer's servers.
Your SFTP credentials and file data remain solely on your device and your own
server. The app communicates with your server exclusively over the SFTP
protocol, which uses SSH encryption to protect data in transit.

### Links to Other Sites

The "Syncore" app does not contain links to other sites.

### Children's Privacy

The "Syncore" app does not require any personal information and is safe for
children to use. The app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable
information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

### Data Retention and Deletion

The "Syncore" app does not collect or retain any personal data. SFTP
credentials stored locally on your device can be deleted at any time by
removing the connection profile within the app, or by uninstalling the app
entirely.

### Your Rights

Since the "Syncore" app does not collect any personal data, there is no data
held by the developer that would be subject to access, correction, or deletion
requests. If you have any privacy-related questions, you are welcome to contact
me at the address below.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review
this page periodically for any changes. I will notify you of any changes by
adding notes in new releases in Google Play Store.
